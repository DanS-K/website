My Stuff

Who I am!

Artist & Coder based in Kent UK

I’m Dan. 80’s enthusiast, retro gamer, artist, vegetable gardener and coding student. I’m on a mission to become a professional programmer and have made this website to document my journey, practice my coding skills and showcase my art.

My step into coding began in 2019 when my father-in-law who works in the field suggested I look into it. I was seeking a career change and this felt like a light bulb turned on in my head! I’m logical, analytical but also creative - programming seemed like the perfect fit for me.

I was diagnosed with rheumatoid arthritis 15 years ago. This had a huge impact on every part of my life and remains a daily challenge for me. I’ve been in a role that works for me physically but doesn’t challenge me enough mentally. With my physical health, I’d always felt restricted with career options but can see that programming will really work for me. Earlier this year, my learning journey really escalated after I was furloughed due to the risks associated with my condition. Every day, I’m learning so much about the way that programming works and I’m absolutely loving it! So far, I’ve gained a great grasp of HTML, CSS and Java Script, I’m now looking at back-end coding starting with Node. One of the things I love the most is when I make something work and truly understand how its happened. I now want to do this more and more!

I’ve made several functioning games and will upload more of these in the coming weeks as I learn more and more. I’ll also upload blog posts to chart my progress and remind me of how far I’ve come on a more challenging day! I’m always looking for new projects, so if you like what you see and would like me to build you a website or send you a piece of my art - please contact me here.
